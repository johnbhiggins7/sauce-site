Frequently asked questions

How do the free AI recipes work?

Every account gets two free AI generations for life. A generation only counts when it succeeds — failed or cancelled attempts are never charged. Regenerating a result counts as another generation.

What does the subscription include?

Sauce AI is $4.99/month or $39.99/year and includes 30 AI recipe generations per monthly window. The annual plan resets monthly (not 360 at once), and unused generations don't roll over. Recipes you've already made stay available even after a subscription ends.

Do I need an account?

You can browse curated recipes and the community, and keep local saves, as a guest. Sign in with Apple is required to create with AI, post, upvote, report, block, or sync.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Manage or cancel anytime in your Apple Account settings → Subscriptions. Apple handles all billing.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open the Sauce AI screen and tap "Restore purchases."

Does Sauce work offline?

Yes — the curated recipe library and your saved recipes work without a connection. AI generation and the community require internet.

How is the community kept safe?

Posts are checked against safety rules before publishing, you can report posts and block accounts, and a moderation team reviews flagged content. AI-assisted posts are always labeled.

How do I report a problem or a post?

Use the "Report a problem" option in a recipe's menu, or email johnbhiggins7@gmail.com.

How do I delete my account or data?

Open Settings → Delete account and confirm. This permanently removes your account, recipes, posts, saves, and votes. See our Privacy Policy for details.
